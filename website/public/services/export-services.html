Our Export Services

At Fission Textiles, we understand that navigating the complexities of international trade can be challenging. Our comprehensive export services are designed to simplify the process, offering end-to-end solutions that handle everything from documentation and compliance to logistics and shipping.

With extensive experience in global textile exports and established relationships with shipping partners and customs authorities, we ensure your products reach their destination efficiently, on time, and in compliance with all relevant regulations.

Export Documentation

We handle all required documentation to ensure smooth customs clearance and regulatory compliance for your textile exports to markets worldwide.

  • Commercial Invoice Preparation
  • Certificate of Origin Documentation
  • Packing Lists and Shipping Manifests
  • Inspection Certificates and Quality Documentation
  • Export Licenses and Permits
  • Electronic Documentation Systems

Logistics & Shipping

Our logistics experts coordinate all aspects of international shipping, selecting the most efficient routes and carriers to meet your timeline and budget requirements.

  • Air Freight Solutions
  • Sea Freight Coordination
  • Multimodal Transport Planning
  • Container Loading and Optimization
  • Tracking and Real-time Updates
  • Last-mile Delivery Coordination

Compliance Management

We ensure your textile exports meet all legal requirements and industry standards in destination countries, minimizing delays and preventing compliance issues.

  • International Trade Regulations Compliance
  • Product Safety and Labeling Requirements
  • Textile-specific Regulations Compliance
  • Import Quota Management
  • Customs Valuation Assistance
  • Rules of Origin Compliance
