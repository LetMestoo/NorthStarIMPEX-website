Our Quality Testing Services

At Fission Textiles, we provide comprehensive quality testing services to ensure your textile products meet international standards, regulatory requirements, and customer expectations. Our state-of-the-art laboratory is equipped with advanced testing equipment and staffed by experienced technicians who conduct rigorous tests according to established protocols.

Whether you need testing for raw materials, in-process verification, or final product validation, our quality testing services offer reliable, accurate results with detailed reports that help you make informed decisions and maintain consistent quality.

Our Testing Methodology

Our quality testing approach is comprehensive, systematic, and tailored to your specific requirements. We follow international standards and employ cutting-edge techniques to deliver accurate, reliable results.

Sample Selection & Preparation

We employ systematic sampling techniques to ensure that test samples accurately represent the entire production batch. Our technicians prepare samples according to standardized procedures to maintain consistency and reliability in testing.

Testing Protocol Implementation

Each test is conducted according to established international standards and protocols such as ASTM, ISO, AATCC, and BS. We calibrate our equipment regularly to ensure precision and follow strict methodologies for consistent results.

Data Analysis & Reporting

Test data is analyzed by experienced professionals who interpret results in the context of international standards and your specific requirements. Our comprehensive reports include detailed findings, pass/fail assessments, and recommendations for improvement where applicable.

Quality Verification

Our quality assurance team conducts secondary verification of all critical test results and regularly participates in inter-laboratory comparison programs to ensure the accuracy and reliability of our testing processes.

Standards We Follow

Our testing procedures are aligned with internationally recognized standards, ensuring that your textile products meet global quality benchmarks and market-specific requirements.
