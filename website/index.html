Production Process

Design & Development

Our experienced design team collaborates with clients to create innovative garment designs that align with current market trends and brand requirements. We utilize advanced CAD software and digital pattern-making technology to ensure precision and efficiency in design development.

Material Sourcing

We partner with certified suppliers to source premium quality fabrics and materials that meet international standards. All materials undergo comprehensive testing for quality, durability, colorfastness, and environmental compliance before production begins.

Cutting & Preparation

Our skilled cutting team utilizes advanced cutting technology and precision equipment to ensure optimal material utilization and minimal waste. Each fabric piece is carefully examined and cut according to exact specifications for consistent, high-quality production.

Manufacturing

Our experienced production team combines traditional garment-making expertise with modern manufacturing technology to create premium quality garments. With our 2,000 pieces per day capacity, we ensure efficient production while maintaining the highest quality standards throughout the manufacturing process.

Quality Control

Every garment undergoes rigorous quality inspection at multiple stages of production. Our dedicated quality control team ensures that each item meets international quality standards, including fabric quality, stitching precision, and finishing details before final packaging.

Shipping & Logistics

We manage complete export documentation, customs compliance, and international shipping logistics to ensure secure and timely delivery to your destination. Our logistics expertise ensures smooth customs clearance and cost-effective transportation solutions for your garment orders.
